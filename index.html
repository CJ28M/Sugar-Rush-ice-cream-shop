About Frosty Delights

Our story of sweet beginnings

Founded in 2023, Frosty Delights began with a simple mission: to create the most delicious, high-quality ice cream using only the finest ingredients. Our founder, Sarah Creamer, started with just three flavors in her home kitchen, and through dedication and passion, we've grown to offer over 31 flavors at multiple locations.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Every scoop is made with love and care, ensuring you get the best ice cream experience possible.
